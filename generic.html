The future
begins in the present

The next and current step of my life starts in Japan.
After living here while serving in the Navy I could not imagine living anywhere else.
With a fascinating culture and amazing scenery I have never regretted my move.
Currently I am attending a Japanese language school and building my skills in order

to find a job and start a new life here in Tokyo.

This has been the hardest and exciting journey of my life. Japanese has proven to be quite the challenge. While here I am learning as much of the language as I can to assist in opening up opportunities for employment.

While studying the language I have been seeking IT related jobs to build my future here in Japan by securing a job that will sponsor me for a work visa. Hard work will pay off and until then I get to spend a lot of time enjoying the food and traveling around the country.
